# KA04: Elicitation and Collaboration - Stakeholders

> **🤖 AI AGENT INSTRUCTION (STAKEHOLDER VERIFICATION):**
> You MUST verify stakeholder participation before conducting or confirming any elicitation activity.
> 
> *   **⚠️ Must Have is Missing:** **BLOCK execution.** For Task 4.2, conducting elicitation without the correct Domain SME or End User means all gathered requirements are invalid hallucinations. If they are absent, you **MUST HALT** and ask the user to reschedule or provide notes from another verified resource.
> *   **Should Have is Missing:** Proceed, but inject a high-priority warning to the user of potential collaboration or technical integration risks.
> *   **Approval Authority is Missing:** Do NOT finalize the corresponding deliverable.

---

## Task 4.1: Prepare for Elicitation
*   **Must Have:**
    *   **Sponsor:** Required to authorize the planned elicitation event and ensure the mandatory participation of specific stakeholders.
    *   **Domain Subject Matter Expert (SME):** Required to provide guidance about which other sources of BA information to consult and to provide supporting materials prior to the event.
*   **Should Have:**
    *   **Project Manager:** Ensures that the appropriate people, facilities, and resources are scheduled and available to conduct the elicitation.
*   **Approval Authority:**
    *   **Sponsor:** Approves the Elicitation Activity Plan (specifically logistics, time, and budget commitments).

## Task 4.2: Conduct Elicitation
*   **Must Have:**
    *   **Domain SME / Customer / End User:** The primary sources of knowledge. Required to actively provide valuable business analysis information, needs, and pain points. Without them, the activity MUST NOT proceed.
*   **Should Have:**
    *   **Implementation SME:** Participates to ask clarifying technical questions and offer early feasibility alternatives.
    *   **Sponsor:** Required to enforce attendance if key stakeholders are resistant or unavailable.
*   **Approval Authority:**
    *   *Not Applicable.* Results captured here are "Unconfirmed". No formal approval is granted during the raw elicitation stage.

## Task 4.3: Confirm Elicitation Results
*   **Must Have:**
    *   **Domain SME / Target Stakeholder:** Required to confirm that the elicitation results correctly reflect their statements and to identify omissions, inconsistencies, or conflicts in the BA's notes.
*   **Should Have:**
    *   **Any stakeholder:** Anyone who participated in Task 4.2 should review the documented results to ensure a shared understanding.
*   **Approval Authority:**
    *   **Domain SME (Confirmation Only):** Acts as the authority to *Confirm* (verify the accuracy of the notes/transcripts), NOT to *Approve* for construction (which is explicitly reserved for Task 5.5).

## Task 4.4: Communicate Business Analysis Information
*   **Must Have:**
    *   **Target Audience (Any Stakeholder):** The specific stakeholders identified in the *Stakeholder Engagement Approach* (Task 3.2) who need to reach a shared understanding of the package's content and implications.
*   **Should Have:**
    *   **Implementation SME & Tester:** Needs to be explicitly aware of the communicated requirements and designs for downstream implementation and testing preparation.
*   **Approval Authority:**
    *   *Not Applicable.* This task focuses on achieving "shared understanding", not securing formal sign-off. (If sign-off is needed, the package is routed to Task 5.5).

## Task 4.5: Manage Stakeholder Collaboration
*   **Must Have:**
    *   **All Key Stakeholders:** Required to explicitly agree to time and resource commitments, and actively engage in ongoing BA activities.
*   **Should Have:**
    *   **Project Manager:** Consulted to help mitigate project-level risks related to stakeholder disengagement or schedule conflicts.
*   **Approval Authority:**
    *   *Not Applicable.* This is an ongoing behavioral monitoring and relationship-building activity.
