# KA03: Business Analysis Planning and Monitoring - Stakeholders

> **🤖 AI AGENT INSTRUCTION (STAKEHOLDER VERIFICATION):**
> You MUST verify stakeholder participation before generating KA03 artifacts.
> *   If a **Must Have** is missing: BLOCK execution and request the user to identify them or a Stakeholder Proxy.
> *   If a **Should Have** is missing: Proceed but WARN the user of a potential quality/compliance drop.
> *   If the **Approval Authority** is missing: Do NOT finalize the deliverable. Demand explicit sign-off routing.

---

## Task 3.1: Plan Business Analysis Approach
*   **Must Have:**
    *   **Sponsor:** Required to provide the initial business needs and objectives that dictate whether the approach should be Predictive or Adaptive.
    *   **Project Manager:** Required to ensure the BA Approach integrates seamlessly into the overall project timeline and resource constraints.
*   **Should Have:**
    *   **Domain Subject Matter Expert (SME):** Needed to assess their availability and willingness to participate in upcoming BA activities.
    *   **Regulator:** Needed to provide constraints if the BA methodology is subject to external compliance audits.
*   **Approval Authority:**
    *   **Sponsor:** Approves the approach to ensure alignment with organizational policies and funding.

## Task 3.2: Plan Stakeholder Engagement
*   **Must Have:**
    *   **Sponsor:** Required to authorize the involvement of specific stakeholders and validate their level of decision-making authority.
    *   **Project Manager:** Shares responsibility for stakeholder identification and manages overall project communication.
*   **Should Have:**
    *   **Domain SME / End User:** Helps identify other hidden stakeholders or informal influencers within the operational units.
    *   **Supplier / Customer:** Needed to map external engagement touchpoints.
*   **Approval Authority:**
    *   **Project Manager & Sponsor:** Joint approval to ensure the communication plan does not conflict with project-level communications.

## Task 3.3: Plan Business Analysis Governance
*   **Must Have:**
    *   **Sponsor:** Required to define the escalation paths and participate as the ultimate decision-maker for scope changes.
    *   **Project Manager:** Required to ensure the BA change control process aligns with the overall project change management process.
*   **Should Have:**
    *   **Regulator:** Identifies rules that must be followed for sound and compliant decision-making and audit trails.
    *   **Domain SME:** May be identified as needing to be involved in change discussions or prioritization workshops.
*   **Approval Authority:**
    *   **Sponsor:** Possesses the ultimate authority to approve the Governance Approach and establish the Change Control Board (CCB).

## Task 3.4: Plan Business Analysis Information Management
*   **Must Have:**
    *   **Sponsor:** Required to review, comment on, and approve how business analysis information will be packaged and stored.
*   **Should Have:**
    *   **Regulator:** Defines mandatory rules related to information data retention, privacy, and traceability compliance.
    *   **Domain SME:** Needed to confirm what specific views of BA information they will require to perform their reviews.
*   **Approval Authority:**
    *   **Sponsor:** Approves the Information Management Approach and any capital expenditures required for BA tools (e.g., Jira, DOORS).

## Task 3.5: Identify Business Analysis Performance Improvements
*   **Must Have:**
    *   **Project Manager:** Accountable for project success; must be consulted before implementing any BA process changes that impact the project schedule.
    *   **Sponsor:** Requires reports on BA performance and authorizes initiatives (e.g., training, new tools) to improve BA efficiency.
*   **Should Have:**
    *   **Domain SME:** Should be informed about changes to BA activities to reset expectations regarding their future involvement.
*   **Approval Authority:**
    *   **Sponsor / BA Center of Excellence (CoE) Lead:** Approves significant process improvements or corrective actions.
