## Task 3.1: Plan Business Analysis Approach
*   **Primary:**
    *   **Estimation:** Used to determine how long it may take to perform business analysis activities based on the selected approach.
    *   **Process Modelling:** Used to define and document the business analysis approach (e.g., mapping out the iterative BA activities).
*   **Common Secondary:**
    *   **Brainstorming / Workshops:** Used collaboratively to identify possible business analysis activities, techniques, and risks to help build the approach.
*   **Other Secondary:** Business Cases, Document Analysis, Financial Analysis, Functional Decomposition, Interviews, Item Tracking, Lessons Learned, Reviews, Risk Analysis and Management, Scope Modelling, Survey or Questionnaire.

## Task 3.2: Plan Stakeholder Engagement
*   **Primary:**
    *   **Stakeholder List, Map, or Personas:** Used to depict the relationship of stakeholders to the solution and to one another, ensuring no key group is overlooked.
    *   **Organizational Modelling:** Used to determine if the organizational units or people listed have any unique needs, interests, or reporting lines that affect collaboration.
*   **Common Secondary:**
    *   **Interviews / Workshops:** Used to directly interact with specific stakeholders or groups to gain more information about their communication and collaboration preferences.
*   **Other Secondary:** Brainstorming, Business Rules Analysis, Document Analysis, Lessons Learned, Mind Mapping, Process Modelling, Risk Analysis and Management, Scope Modelling, Survey or Questionnaire.

## Task 3.3: Plan Business Analysis Governance
*   **Primary:**
    *   **Organizational Modelling:** Used to understand roles and responsibilities within the organization in an effort to define a governance approach that involves the right stakeholders for approvals.
    *   **Process Modelling:** Used to document the step-by-step process or method for governing business analysis (e.g., Change Control Process).
*   **Common Secondary:**
    *   **Document Analysis / Reviews:** Used to evaluate existing corporate governance processes or templates, and to validate the proposed governance plan with key stakeholders.
*   **Other Secondary:** Brainstorming, Interviews, Item Tracking, Lessons Learned, Survey or Questionnaire, Workshops.

## Task 3.4: Plan Business Analysis Information Management
*   **Primary:**
    *   **Mind Mapping:** Used to identify and visually categorize the kinds of information and artifacts that need to be managed and traced.
    *   **Process Modelling:** Used to document the process or method for managing business analysis information (e.g., how requirements flow from 'Draft' to 'Approved' in Jira).
*   **Common Secondary:**
    *   **Interviews / Workshops:** Used to help stakeholders uncover their specific business analysis information management needs (e.g., C-level needs dashboards, testers need traceability matrices).
*   **Other Secondary:** Brainstorming, Item Tracking, Lessons Learned, Survey or Questionnaire.

## Task 3.5: Identify Business Analysis Performance Improvements
*   **Primary:**
    *   **Metrics and Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):** Used to determine what metrics are appropriate for assessing BA performance (e.g., defect rate, timeliness) and how they may be tracked.
    *   **Root Cause Analysis:** Used to help identify the underlying cause of failures or difficulties in accomplishing business analysis work (e.g., why approvals are always delayed).
    *   **Process Analysis:** Used to analyze existing business analysis processes and identify opportunities for improvement.
*   **Common Secondary:**
    *   **Lessons Learned / Reviews:** Used to identify recommended changes to business analysis processes, templates, and assets that can be incorporated into future work.
*   **Other Secondary:** Brainstorming, Interviews, Item Tracking, Observation, Process Modelling, Risk Analysis and Management, Survey or Questionnaire, Workshops.
