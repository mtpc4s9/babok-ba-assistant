# KA06: Strategy Analysis - Stakeholders

> **🤖 AI AGENT INSTRUCTION (STAKEHOLDER VERIFICATION):**
> You MUST verify stakeholder participation before generating KA06 artifacts. Strategy Analysis requires high-level executive (C-level) and Sponsor involvement.
> *   If a **Must Have** is missing: BLOCK execution and request the user to identify them or a Stakeholder Proxy.
> *   If a **Should Have** is missing: Proceed but WARN the user of a potential quality/compliance drop.
> *   If the **Approval Authority** is missing: Do NOT finalize the deliverable. Demand explicit sign-off routing.

## Task 6.1: Analyze Current State
*   **Must Have:**
    *   **Sponsor:** Required to provide the executive context for the performance of existing solutions and articulate the high-level business need.
    *   **Domain Subject Matter Expert (SME):** Required to provide deep insight into the actual current state operations, uncovering root causes rather than just symptoms.
*   **Should Have:**
    *   **Operational Support:** Provides critical ground-level information regarding operational pain points and existing system limitations.
*   **Approval Authority:**
    *   **Sponsor:** Approves the Current State Description to ensure the baseline is accurate before investing in future state design.

## Task 6.2: Define Future State
*   **Must Have:**
    *   **Sponsor:** Required to determine which business needs to address, set the strategic business objectives, and ensure funding is available.
    *   **Domain SME:** Required to provide realistic insight into potential future states and validate if the desired capabilities are achievable.
*   **Should Have:**
    *   **Regulator:** Ensures that the desired future state complies with existing or upcoming laws, regulations, and industry rules.
    *   **Customer:** Represents the ultimate consumer of the future state; their needs ensure the future state actually delivers market value.
*   **Approval Authority:**
    *   **Sponsor:** Approves the Business Objectives and Future State Description to align with the enterprise's overarching corporate strategy.

## Task 6.3: Assess Risks
*   **Must Have:**
    *   **Sponsor:** Required to evaluate the risk analysis results against the organization's risk tolerance and authorize budget for risk mitigation.
    *   **Domain SME:** Required to identify hidden operational or business risks based on their specific area of expertise.
*   **Should Have:**
    *   **Implementation SME:** Provides technical and architectural insight into risks associated with transitioning to the future state.
    *   **Regulator:** Identifies compliance, legal, or security risks that could result in severe penalties or loss of reputation.
*   **Approval Authority:**
    *   **Sponsor:** Must accept the residual risks and approve the risk treatment strategy (Avoid, Mitigate, Transfer, Accept, Increase).

## Task 6.4: Define Change Strategy
*   **Must Have:**
    *   **Sponsor:** Required to champion the change, authorize the approach (e.g., build vs. buy), and ensure enterprise readiness.
    *   **Domain SME:** Required to assess the operational impact of the change strategy and evaluate transition states.
*   **Should Have:**
    *   **Project Manager:** Responsible for translating the selected change strategy into an executable project plan and schedule.
    *   **Implementation SME:** Assesses the technical feasibility and transition architecture of the proposed alternative change strategies.
*   **Approval Authority (⚠️ MANDATORY):**
    *   **Sponsor:** The Sponsor MUST be the final Approval Authority for the Change Strategy and Solution Scope. No strategic initiative can proceed to execution or detailed requirements analysis (KA07) without explicit Sponsor sign-off on the financial and strategic investment.
