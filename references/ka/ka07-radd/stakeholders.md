# KA07: Requirements Analysis and Design Definition - Stakeholders

> **🤖 AI AGENT INSTRUCTION (STAKEHOLDER VERIFICATION):**
> You MUST verify stakeholder participation before executing KA07 tasks.
> * If a **Must Have** is missing: BLOCK execution. ⚠️ For 7.2/7.3, lacking a Domain SME means requirements cannot be verified or validated. For 7.6, lacking a Sponsor means the solution cannot be recommended.
> * If the **Approval Authority** is missing: Do NOT finalize the recommendation. Demand explicit sign-off.

---

## Task 7.1: Specify and Model Requirements

*   **Must Have:**
    *   **Any Stakeholder:** The BA can perform this independently or invite any stakeholder. However, Domain SMEs and Implementation SMEs typically provide the core inputs for models.
*   **Approval Authority:** *Not Applicable* (This is purely a generation/drafting task).

## Task 7.2: Verify Requirements

*   **Must Have:**
    *   **Domain SME & Implementation SME:** Share the *primary responsibility* with the BA to ensure the requirements meet quality standards (Atomic, Testable, Consistent).
*   **Approval Authority:** *Not Applicable* (This is a quality control check, not a formal business sign-off).

## Task 7.3: Validate Requirements

*   **Must Have:**
    *   **Customer, End User, & Sponsor:** Share the *primary responsibility* to ensure the requirements align with Business Objectives and deliver actual business value.
*   **Approval Authority:** **Sponsor / Customer** (They must confirm that the specified needs are worth investing in).

## Task 7.4: Define Requirements Architecture

*   **Must Have:**
    *   **Domain SME & Implementation SME:** Required to ensure the business viewpoints and technical viewpoints fit together seamlessly.
    *   **Tester:** Required to ensure the architecture allows for comprehensive quality assurance and testability.
*   **Approval Authority:** *Not Applicable* (Focuses on structuring information, not authorizing construction).

## Task 7.5: Define Design Options

*   **Must Have:**
    *   **Implementation SME:** Crucial for providing technical constraints and estimating the costs/effort of different options.
    *   **Domain SME:** Required to assess the business benefits of each proposed design alternative.
*   **Approval Authority:** *Not Applicable* (Options are generated and evaluated here, but the final decision is strictly reserved for Task 7.6).

## Task 7.6: Analyze Potential Value and Recommend Solution

*   **Must Have:**
    *   **Sponsor:** The ultimate decision-maker; must be kept informed of changes in potential value, risks, and opportunity costs.
    *   **Project Manager:** Needs to understand the selected option to manage the project scope and plan the deployment schedule.
*   **Should Have:**
    *   **Regulator:** Required to assess any legal or compliance risks associated with the recommended solution.
*   **Approval Authority (⚠️ MANDATORY):**
    *   **Sponsor:** Holds the ultimate authority to approve the expenditure of resources and explicitly sign off on the Final Solution Recommendation.
